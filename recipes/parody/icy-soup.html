Icy Soup

Description

DISCLAIMER: This recipe is a parody, so please do not take this seriously. Anyhow, I hope you have a good laugh :3

Are you broke? Here is an affordable meal that is also healthy for you!

Ingredients

  • Table that can handle the weight of your tears
  • Empty bowl
  • 1 1/2 cups of ice
  • Handful of optimism
  • Pinch of discipline
  • Teaspoon of dreams

Instructions

  1. Cry.
  2. While crying, set the bowl on the table.
  3. In the bowl, toss in the optimism and dreams. Mix them together until it feels stiff.
  4. Put the ice into the bowl. Shake until the ice seems well coated.
  5. Garnish the bowl with the dreams.
  6. Enjoy your meal :D
